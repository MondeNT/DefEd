About Deaf Ed Tech

Deaf Ed Tech is a transformative platform designed to bridge the education gap for Deaf learners. Our mission is to provide accessible, engaging, and empowering solutions that foster inclusivity and innovation.

Supported by the Technology Innovation Agency, our work has been recognized nationally as one of South Africa's top social innovations. We aim to uplift marginalized communities and position South Africa as a global leader in inclusive, technology-driven education.

  • Empowering Deaf learners with cutting-edge tools
  • Recognized among South Africa's Top 30 Innovations
  • Driving social change through education
