How to Play - Speed Challenge

Test your speed and accuracy by matching the number on the screen with the correct hand gesture before time runs out!

How to Play:

  • A number will appear on the screen.
  • Use your hand to make the correct sign representing that number.
  • Make sure your hand is clearly visible to the camera.
  • Earn points for every correct match before the timer runs out.
  • The faster you are, the higher your score!
